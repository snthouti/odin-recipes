Maggi

Making Maggi in 2 minutes

Maggi is an instant noodles bran in India and it sells instant noodles for as much less money as 5 rupees. It can be made in just 2 minutes and doesn't require any Ingredients from home to make it. It is tasty to eat and doesn't cost much. It usually just takes water and gas to make a maggi and you can enjoy it whereever you want.

Ingredients

  • Maggi packet
  • Water
  • Gas

Steps:

  1. Heat water in a cooking pan
  2. Tear the Maggi packet and mix the Maggi masala in the boiling water
  3. Now break the maggi noodles into two small pieces and put them into boiling water
  4. Mix the noodles and close the lid for 3 minutes
  5. Serve Maggi hot with whatever you want
