Dosa

The South Indian Dosa

Dosa is South Indian Dish made from Rice batter. It is very famous in the whole southern India and is also getting noticed in the Northern Part of India. It is usually eaten with Thick Potato curry, chatni and Sambar.

There are several different types of Dosas found in India. Masala Dosa, Benne Dosa, Set Dosa, Neer dosa being some of the famous ones. There is also a recent hype of mixing modern dishes with Dosa like Paneer Dosa, Cheese dosa or Chocolate Dosa. There is a lot of backlash for people dishes like this in the souther part of India. They claim their traiditional dish is being ruined by excessive use of Cheese in everything.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Potatoz
  • Coconut
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. First Mixture all the batter until fine and thick
  2. Add salt and pepper and mix the batter until it's all good and mixed up
  3. Boil the potatoes and peel them
  4. Add saasvi, jeergi, onion, pepper and all the shit to make the tadka
  5. Now add the mashed potatoes in the cooking pan to make the curry
  6. Now use a Dosa maker and spread the Dosa batter on the pan (Remember to keep the flame on medium, else the batter will end up sticking to the pan before you can spread the dosa batter around the pan)
  7. Cook the batter until Dosa is neat and clean and serve hot with potato curry.
